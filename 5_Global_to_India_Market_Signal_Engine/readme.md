# Global to India Market Signal Engine

## Business Case
1. Tracks global market movements.
2. Measures how internation events impact Indian markets.
3. Correlates US, European and Asian markets.
4. Monitors commodities, currencies and bond yeilds.
5. Generates early warning signals.
6. Helps investors prepare before Indian markets open.
7. Supports macroeconomic analysis.
8. Identifies market sentiment shifts.
9. Improves timing of investment decisions.
10. Provides context behind market movements.
